 Johnson college is an autonomous engineering college located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It was founded on 17 October 1984. It is an ISO9001:2008 Certified Institution.It is sponsored by the tamilien Charities, a social welfare organization of the Johnson and Johnson company, U.S and its German collaborator, John. The college was affiliated with Madurai Kamaraj University, Madurai till 2002 and later with Anna University, Chennai, but since 2012 it has been affiliated with Anna University of Technology, Chennai.
